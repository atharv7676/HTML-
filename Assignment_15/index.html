Dosa

Ready to taste?

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is thin, crispy, and served hot with coconut chutney, sambar, and sometimes potato masala filling.

Jolad Rotti

Ready to taste?

Jolad Rotti is a traditional North Karnataka flatbread made from jowar flour. It is soft and healthy, usually served with spicy curries, chutneys, vegetables, traditional thali meal.

Biriyani

Ready to taste?

Biryani is a famous Indian rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice, spices, herbs, and meat or vegetables. It is slow-cooked for rich flavor and usually served with raita, salad, or boiled eggs.

Misal-Pav

Ready to taste?

Misal Pav is a spicy Maharashtrian dish made with sprouted lentil curry called misal. It is topped with crunchy farsan, onions, and coriander, and served hot with soft pav bread.

Food is any substance that people or animals eat or drink to get energy and nutrients. It helps the body grow, repair tissues, and maintain good health and proper body functions.

Food provides energy for daily activities and supplies nutrients that help the body grow, repair tissues, and maintain a healthy immune system.

The main nutrients in food are carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water which help the body stay healthy and active.

A balanced diet contains the right amounts of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and fiber needed for proper body growth and health.

Fruits and vegetables provide vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and fiber that help improve digestion and strengthen the immune system.

Junk food is food that contains high sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats but very little nutritional value.

Too much junk food can cause obesity, heart disease, diabetes, and other health problems.

Vegetarian food includes plant-based foods such as fruits, vegetables, grains, nuts, and dairy products but does not include meat or fish.

Non-vegetarian food includes meat, fish, eggs, and poultry which are rich in proteins and nutrients.

Breakfast provides energy after a long night without food and helps improve concentration and metabolism.

Proteins help build and repair body tissues and muscles and are essential for growth.

Carbohydrates are the main source of energy for the body and help the brain function properly.

Vitamins are nutrients that help maintain body functions and protect the body from diseases.

Water helps regulate body temperature, aids digestion, transports nutrients, and removes waste.

Organic food is produced without synthetic chemicals or pesticides and is considered environmentally friendly.

Fast food is food that is prepared and served quickly such as burgers, fries, and sandwiches.

Washing fruits removes dirt, bacteria, and pesticide residues that may be present.

Healthy food contains nutrients that support body growth, energy, and overall health.

Eating food on time helps maintain proper digestion and stable energy levels.

Traditional food represents the culture and cooking style of a particular region.
